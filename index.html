About

The IEEE IAS Student Branch Chapter of Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology (RIT), Kottayam, is the first of its kind in Asia Pacific Region R-10. The chapter was formally inaugurated by Dr. Mukul Chandorkar, Professor, Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in May 2010. The chapter made its humble start with 6 student members all belonging to the EE department, Industrial Drives and Control. With consistent support from Dr. Tomy Sebastian,Chapter Mentor and Dr.Vincent George, Chapter Advisor, the IAS chapter, with its viable activities has been able to make an indelible impact on its members as well as in the institution. Ms. Aathira K.V. (2010-2011), Mr. Srikanth V. Pillai (2011-2012) and Ms. Lina Sera Varghese (2012-2013) have been three successful chapter chairs since 2010. Later Mr. Srikanth V. Pillai was appointed as R-10 SB IAS Chapters Area Chair in 2012 and as CMD SB Chapters Development Committee Chair in 2013. Having won many prestigious recognitions, yet humble, the chapter is moving forward with its aim set high.

Vision

To be a world leader in the advancement of technology and dissemination of technical information; to support professionals engaged in the applications of electrical and electronic engineering in the industry; the advancement of the theory and practice of electrical and electronic engineering, from the development, design, manufacture and application of electrical systems, apparatus, devices and controls, to the processes and equipment of industry and commerce; the promotion of safe, reliable and economical installations; industry leadership in energy conservation and environmental health and safety issues; the creation of voluntary engineering standards and recommended practices and the professional development of its members.
