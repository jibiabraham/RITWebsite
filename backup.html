About IEEE & IAS

IEEE is the world's largest professional association devoted to technological advancement for the benefit of humanity. IEEE, through its high-standard publications, conferences, professional and educational activities, has instilled tremendous inspiration in the whole global community. IEEE has 38 technical societies which enables its members to get connected with breakthrough information of their own technical fields. One among these, is the IEEE Industrial Applications Society, which has a great legacy.

IAS Vision

To be a world leader in the advancement of technology and dissemination of technical information, to support professionals engaged in the applications of electrical and electronic engineering in the industry.The advancement of the theory and practice of electrical and electronic engineering, from the development, design, manufacture and application of electrical systems, apparatus, devices and controls, to the processes and equipment of industry and commerce; the promotion of safe, reliable and economical installations; industry leadership in energy conservation and environmental health and safety issues; the creation of voluntary engineering standards and recommended practices; and the professional development of its members.

About IAS @ RIT

The IAS Students branch chapter was formally inaugurated on 13th august 2010 by Dr. Mukul Chandorkar , Professor IIT Bombay. The Chapter was started with an initial strength of 6 student members.

The Chapter Today

Today, the chapter functions with 20 active student members. The student strength and support is expected to grow in future.
